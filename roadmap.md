# Purpose

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of ARGOS from its initial concept to a mature Cognitive Operating System.

The roadmap provides long-term direction while remaining flexible enough to adapt as new technologies, research, and requirements emerge.

---

# Vision

To build ARGOS into a trustworthy, modular, and extensible Cognitive Operating System that enhances human productivity through intelligent reasoning, memory, planning, and automation.

---

# Development Philosophy

The roadmap is guided by the following principles:

* Architecture before implementation.
* Incremental development.
* Documentation-driven engineering.
* Model independence.
* Human-first design.
* Continuous learning.

# Future Directions

Potential areas of expansion include:

* Vision system
* Mobile companion application
* IoT integration
* Smart home automation
* Multi-device synchronization
* Robotics interfaces
* Plugin ecosystem
* Multi-user support
* Advanced memory management
* Local-first intelligence

These features are intentionally outside the current MVP and will be evaluated after the core platform is stable.

---

# Success Metrics

ARGOS will be considered successful when:

* It is used regularly in daily workflows.
* The architecture remains understandable.
* New features integrate without major redesign.
* Documentation stays synchronized with development.
* The platform remains independent of any single AI provider.

---

# Guiding Principle

Progress is measured by engineering quality, reliability, and maintainability—not by the number of implemented features.

---

# Roadmap Review

This roadmap should be reviewed at the completion of each major milestone.

Adjustments should be documented through Engineering Decision Records to preserve the project's engineering history.

---

# Summary

ARGOS is envisioned as a long-term engineering platform rather than a short-term software project.

The roadmap serves as a strategic guide, ensuring that each milestone builds upon a strong architectural foundation while keeping the long-term vision clear.
